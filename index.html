The Crystal Game

Instructions:


1. The computer will randomly generate a number between 20 and 120 for your target.


2. The computer will randomly assign a value between 1 and 12 to each crystal. New values will be generated for each new game.


3. You will not know the value of each crystal until you click on it and see the incremental increase in your total. Each time you click on a crystal, your total will be increased by the value of that crystal.


4. To win, you must find a combination of the crystals' values which add to exactly the target value. You lose when you exceed the target value.
